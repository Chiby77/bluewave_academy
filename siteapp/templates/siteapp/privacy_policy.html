Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data.

Last updated: February 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Bluewave Academy ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website and the educational platform. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

Types of Data Collected:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your full name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Profile Information: Educational background, field of study, learning goals, and profile picture.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including exam attempts, scores, and course progress.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies to enhance your experience and track your preferences.
  • Communication Data: Messages, support tickets, and communications you send to us.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address.

3. Use of Data

Bluewave Academy uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features of our platform
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our service
  • To monitor the usage of our platform
  • To detect, prevent and address technical and security issues
  • To provide you with news, special offers, and general information unless you have opted not to receive such communications

4. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Disclosure of Data

Bluewave Academy may disclose your personal data in the following situations:

  • By Law
  • By Court Order
  • With Your Consent
  • For Business Transfers: If Bluewave Academy is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

6. Cookies

We use cookies to collect information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our service.

7. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to other sites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

8. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our service or to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 18 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from children without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
